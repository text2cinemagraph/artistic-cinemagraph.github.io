Ablation - Importance of Twin Image Generation

We study the role of twin image synthesis. We compare our full method, which predicts optical flow on the realistic image, to Ours (w/o twin), which directly predicts optical flow using the artistic image. The results show that our predicted flow (using a realistic image) is significantly smoother and consistent.
